    \subsection{Problem 1 : Solving systems of
ODEs}\label{problem-1-solving-systems-of-odes}

You are given an Isothermal Plug Flow Reactor in which components A and
C are fed in equimolar amounts, and the follow- ing reaction takes place
\(2 A \to B\), with \(C\) as a third non-reacting component. You assume
that the reaction takes place in the liquid phase and that the
volumetric flow rate remains constant even when reaction occurs.

The equations for all three species in the plug flow reactor are:

\(u \dfrac{dC_i}{dz} =r_j\), j = 1,...,3

\(C_A\) (0) = 2 kmol/m3, \(C_B\) (0) = 0, \(C_C\) (0) = 2 kmol/m\^{}3
and we take u = 0.5 m/s, k = 0.3 m\^{}3/kmol s, and the total reactor
length as z = 2.4 m.

\begin{enumerate}
\def\labelenumi{\arabic{enumi}.}
\tightlist
\item
  Find and plot:
\end{enumerate}

\(C_A(z), C_B(z), C_C(z)\)
